Predicting house prices is one of the biggest problems in the Real Estate industry. Using the 'Miami House Prediction' dataset we shall go over some of the main techniques which I have used to solve this problem.

My Approach towards this problem

The main features used while solving this problem of predicting the prices of houses are Latitude, Longitude, Parcel No., Land Square Foot, Floor Area, Value of Special Features, Distance to nearest Railway, Distance to nearest Ocean, Distance to central business district, Distance to nearest subcenter, Distance to nearest highway, Age of the structure, Sale month, Quality of Structure.

I have tried various models to predict the price of houses like Linear Regession, Logistic Regression, Decision Tree Regreesion, Random Forest, XGBoost and Neural Network. I got best results from Xgboost i.e. a score of 93%. While preprocessing the dataset, I observed that the distribution of data points of prices were left skewed. So, I took log of all the prices and it resulted in normal distribution. This also helped in decreasing the MSE.
